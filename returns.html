Returns Policy

Last updated: June 2026

14-Day Return Window

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, you may return your item within 14 days of receiving it.

Conditions for Return

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Items must be in the original packaging
  • A proof of purchase (order confirmation email) must be provided
  • Items that have been used, damaged, or altered will not be accepted

Return Postage

Customers are responsible for paying the cost of return postage. We recommend using a tracked delivery service as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 5 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Please note that your bank or card provider may take additional time to reflect the refund in your account.

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please email us at icewindfan@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll reply within 1 business day with return instructions.

Faulty or Damaged Items

If you receive a faulty or damaged item, please contact us immediately at icewindfan@gmail.com. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return postage.
