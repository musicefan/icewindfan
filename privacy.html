Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ice Wind Fan ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and store your information in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

Who We Are

Ice Wind Fan is a UK-based e-commerce business selling portable handheld fans. You can contact us at icewindfan@gmail.com.

What Data We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)

Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your personal data solely for the purpose of:

  • Processing and fulfilling your order
  • Sending order confirmation and delivery updates
  • Handling returns or customer service queries

We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

We Will Never Sell Your Data

Your personal information will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with trusted service providers who help us fulfil your order (such as Stripe for payments and courier services for delivery), and only to the extent necessary.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your order information for up to 6 years for accounting and legal purposes, in line with UK law. After this period, your data is securely deleted.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at icewindfan@gmail.com.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for the site to function (such as your shopping cart). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Security

All payments are processed through Stripe, which is PCI DSS compliant. Your card details are never stored on our servers.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at icewindfan@gmail.com.
